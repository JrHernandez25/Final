Welcome To the WILD!

In this interactive web game you will have total control of choice. Each new page will contain four new choices that you the player will chose from. Keep in mind that while picking each choice will have a differnt out come but also that you have to pick something quickly because you have only 12 seconds to choose. If you take longer than those 12 seconds given the game will automacticly choose one for you, and you don't know which one it will pick.
